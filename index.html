About Us

Brainfuze is an IIT Delhi-based startup aiming to prepare students for Boards and competitive exams like JEE, NEET, BITSAT, KVPY and Olympiads. We provide one-on-one classes to students at a reasonable cost. All the lessons are given by IITians who themselves have aced their exams and are very passionate about teaching.

Brainfuze aims at developing students interest in studying and laying a solid foundation for their careers. Every student is different, and a true teacher can help channel students' interests in the right direction. One-on-one classes aim at providing personalized classes for students to help them study as per their pace and interests.
